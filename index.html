Men's Over the Calf Dress Socks Merino Wool Knee High Calf Socks

  • 1 pair of Comfortable, elegant, and durable socks for business, formal, or casual wear

  • Designed with 3% spandex, comfortably keeping the socks up above your calf all day long
  • Proudly knit in the USA using only the finest yarns
  • Reinforced heel and toe for durability

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Our merino wool socks are a staple for the professional man's wardrobe. Featuring solid colors knit with a vertical rib, these socks are appropriate for both formal and casual settings. Merino wool is a soft, natural fiber that enables your feet to breathe, providing daylong comfort in warm and cool weather. The top band of the socks are designed with just the right amount of high strength spandex, ensuring that the socks remain up on your leg throughout the work day while still maintaining a comfortable fit. Machine wash warm, tumble dry low. Proudly knit in North Carolina, USA.
